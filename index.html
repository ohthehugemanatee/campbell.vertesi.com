Classical Music Evangelist

Some of the greatest, most powerful, most fun music ever composed is presented in an unpleasant and exclusive way. Through work with the opera band The Cast and other revolutionary classical groups, I strive to bring this music to a broader audience. Because music is for everyone.

Open Source Evangelist

Through the Drupal community, I help to spread open source values to an ever-wider community. I help people discover how they can help with the software that is changing the world. Because everyone can contribute.

Technical Architect and Strategist

I help bring the open source values that run your website into the rest of your organization. I work with non-profit and government organizations like the United Way and OxFam International, because together, we can do more.

Other stuff

I live in Cologne, Germany, but I seem to spend half my time on airplanes to other exciting places. When I get spare time I study classical Tae Kwon Do and Bujinkan Ninjutsu. I go camping with my friends, and on adventures with my family.
